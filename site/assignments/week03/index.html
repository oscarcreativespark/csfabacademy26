Computer-Controlled Cutting

Laser Cutter Characterisations¶

Epilog Fusion PRO{#epilog-fusion-pro}¶

  • Focus: 2" Lens
  • Power: 80W
  • Speed: Max. processing speed: 4.32 m/s. The software presents each material speed as a % of this value.
  • Rate: 1-60 kHz, depending on the settings for that particular material. Timber and Card are at the lower end, and plastics like Acrylic seem to be at the higher end.
  • Kerf: 0.4mm
  • Joint Clearance: 0.2mm
  • Type: CO2
