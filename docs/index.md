## Welcome to our node site!

We are a technical prototyping platform based at [Creative Spark](https://creativespark.ie/) in Dundalk, Co. Louth, Ireland. Our 200+ sq. meter facility is fully equipped with digital fabrication machinery, power tools, and dedicated workspaces to support makers, designers, and entrepreneurs.
